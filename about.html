About me (Jake Ruff)

Born and raised in Houston, TX from a young age I always loved computers and technology. Whether it was modding video games, filming and editing videos with my friends or taking years of 3D animation classes, if I was in front of a computer I was having a great time.

I studied a Texas State University in San Marcos, TX with a Bachelor of Science in Digital Media Innovation & Mass Communication. There I learned about a wide breadth of skills and a digital entrepreneurship mindset. I was exposed to web development, digital marketing, media design, and user experience design. It allowed me to combine my creative skills and love of technology.

After graduation, I moved back to Houston and am now working at Method21, a web development and digital marketing agency located here in town.

When I'm not working, I enjoy spending quality time with my friends, family and girlfriend, playing games (I'm a Halo fanatic), creating music and eating great food!
